Experiment of temperature gradient metamorphism

A 3 weeks long experiment of temperature gradient metamorphism was run at Col de Porte, French Alps, in order to provide precise microtomographic 3-D data for the validation of temperature gradient metamorphism models.
Thanks to X-ray microtomographic images of snow samples obtained under different temperature gradient conditions, it is possible to access important 3-D geometrical information such as the curvature field or the specific surface area of the considered samples. These measurements provide quantitative data, allowing a better comprehension of the metamorphism processes
