Mean Curvature

The mean curvature is a fundamental parameter for snow metamorphisms. With our method, we can obtain the 3-D curvature on each voxel of the object's surface.

Further reading:

  • Flin, F., J.-B. Brzoska, B. Lesaffre, C. Coléou and R. A. Pieritz, 2004. Three-dimensional geometric measurements of snow microstructural evolution under isothermal conditions, Ann. Glaciol., 38, 39-44, (10.3189/172756404781814942).

  • Brzoska, J.-B., B. Lesaffre, C. Coléou, K. Xu and R. A. Pieritz, 1999. Computation of 3D curvatures on a wet snow sample, Eur. Phys. J. AP, 7, 45-57, (10.1051/epjap:1999198).
